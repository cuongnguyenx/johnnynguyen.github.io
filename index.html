Research

I'm broadly interested in understanding and designing safer and fairer interactions within algorithm-mediated communication systems (e.g. social media, LLM-based chat). My work leverages methods from natural language processing (NLP), social computing and human computer interaction (HCI). I'm currently particularly excited about the following research directions

  • Anti-sociality in Online Communities [COMPASS 2023, CHI VSP 2023, ICLR PML4DC 2022]
  • NLP + Health [JMIR MH 2022]
  • Human-Centered AI [CHI 2024]
